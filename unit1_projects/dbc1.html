Why Brains Are Awesome

Brains

The brain is a remarkable three pound organ that controls all aspects of being human,
including decision-making, dreaming, feeling, language, and voluntary movement.

There are 100 billion neurons in the brain. They communicate with one another through electrochemical impulses.
The brain is connected to every part of the body through the Peripheral Nervous System. Each part of the body is represented
in a specific location in the somatosensory cortex. If a neuroscientist activates a spot on the somatosensory
cortex, you will experience a sensation in the corresponding body part.





    Some Random Facts About the Brain:
  • People who've had a hemispherectomy (half their brain removed) have led normal lives.
  • When a person is in love, the reward centers of the brain are activated in the same way that they are activated in drug addicts.
  • The brain itself can not feel pain, which allows neurosurgeons to operate on patients while they are awake.
    Some of the Coolest Parts of the Brain:
  1. Cerebrum: comprising 85% of the brain, the cerebrum allows us to think and control our voluntary muscles.
  2. Right Temporal Lobe: when a seizure occurs in this area, people often experience religious ecstasy.
  3. Medial Prefrontal Cortex: this part of the brain is more active when being creative, while the dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (a decision-making area) is less active, allowing us to focus on self-expression and improvization.
